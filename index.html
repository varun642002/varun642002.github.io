Impact of Agricultural Machinery Adoption on Crop Returns

Economic Analysis Using ROI Framework

Conducted an empirical research study examining the financial impact of agricultural machinery adoption on crop returns using primary data collected from 166 farming households. Applied descriptive statistics, Pearson correlation, reliability analysis, multiple regression, and hypothesis testing.

166 Farming Households
0.7789 Cronbach's Alpha
84.94% Positive ROI Impact
83% Yield Improvement

Key Research Findings

  • 84.94% of respondents reported a positive ROI impact following machinery adoption.
  • 83% reported improvements in crop yield.
  • Fuel-cost impact emerged as the strongest predictor of ROI in correlation analysis.
  • High machinery purchase cost was identified as the largest adoption barrier.
